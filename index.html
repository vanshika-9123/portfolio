Get to know me!

Hi! I am Vanshika Bachkaniwala , an aspiring Multi-media designer . I am currently studying at Humber College . I thrive on creativity and enjoy exploring the intersection of technology, design, and storytelling.

In my free time, you'll often find me sketching away in my notebook or immersing myself in the world of movies. One of my favorite pastimes is exploring new cafes around the city, where I find inspiration in the ambiance, flavors, and conversations that unfold. I believe in the power of human connection and the importance of cultivating meaningful experiences in both work and leisure. Feel free to contact me here.
